    Rescued Date: August 29, 2020
    Given Name:Lolo Marco
    Sex: Male
    Age: 12 years old

    One of the benefits of spaying is behavior change, less aggression. Just look at
    Marco's transformation. Dirty, many wounds because he was always fighting back then.
    It was 6 years after we were able to trapped and neuter him. It is worth the effort
    and to gain his trust and confidence. The habit does not change immediately but the
    Kapon effect is good even physically. Now Marco looks like another cat. You have no
    trace of aggression on him even if you carry or caress him. His back wound was healed.

    Marco was spayed and also have the vaccine. He is now an outdoor cat.
